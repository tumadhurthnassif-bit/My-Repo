Strategic Investment in Employee Technical Competency

Program Scope

Four specialized technical tracks spanning 12-16 training days, designed for technical staff and key personnel requiring specialized knowledge in infrastructure and communication systems.

Expected Outcomes

  • Enhanced technical capabilities and operational efficiency
  • Stronger infrastructure management skills
  • Reduced external consulting costs
  • Faster problem resolution
  • Improved system reliability

Strategic Value

This training investment develops internal expertise in critical infrastructure areas, reducing dependency on external consultants and enabling faster, more cost-effective problem resolution. It strengthens organizational capability and supports long-term growth and competitiveness.

Four Specialized Training Tracks

Track 1: Servers & Data Center Fundamentals (4 days)

Data center architecture, server technologies, environmental systems, capacity planning. Business application: Improved infrastructure planning, better system selection, optimized operations.

Track 2: IP Phones & Yeastar PBX Systems (3 days)

VoIP technology, Yeastar systems, BOQ preparation, cost analysis. Business application: Cost-effective communication systems, professional proposals, successful implementations.

Track 3: CCTV & FTTX Solutions (3 days)

Network topology design, signal loss calculations, equipment selection. Business application: Professional security system design, optimized network infrastructure, reduced signal loss.

Track 4: Network Switches (3 days)

Switch fundamentals, types, features, selection criteria. Business application: Optimized network performance, better infrastructure planning, improved reliability.
